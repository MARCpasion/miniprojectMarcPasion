PAMULINAWEN FESTIVAL

The Pamulinawen Festival is a vibrant annual celebration in Laoag City, Ilocos Norte, held every February to honor Saint William the Hermit, the city’s patron saint. Named after a beloved Ilocano folk song, the festival showcases the rich culture and traditions of the Ilocano people through colorful street dances, parades, and religious activities, making it a time of unity, faith, and joy.
